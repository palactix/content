---

# **1. What Are Cookies?**

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
They help the website remember your actions and preferences, so your experience stays smooth.

Cookies are **not** harmful and do **not** give us access to your computer or personal files.

---

# **2. How We Use Cookies**

We use cookies to:

* Keep you logged in
* Remember your preferences
* Improve speed and performance
* Analyze how users interact with our pages
* Enhance security
* Support features like scheduling, media uploads, and dashboard settings

Palactix only uses cookies that are necessary to operate the platform and improve user experience.

# **4. How to Control Cookies**

You can control or delete cookies anytime.

### **Browser Settings**

You can block or disable cookies directly in:

* Chrome
* Firefox
* Safari
* Edge
* Brave
* Any other modern browser

Doing this may limit or break certain features of Palactix.

### **Opt-Out Tools**

You may also disable certain tracking technologies using standard opt-out platforms depending on your region.
