**GDPR Compliance Statement - Palactix**
*Last Updated: 17/11/2025*

Palactix is fully committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) and protecting the privacy and rights of users located in the European Union (EU), the European Economic Area (EEA), and the United Kingdom (UK).

This page explains how Palactix collects, processes, stores, and protects personal data in accordance with GDPR requirements.

---

# **1. Our Commitment to GDPR**

We are committed to:

* Collecting only the data we need
* Being transparent about how we use it
* Giving you control over your personal data
* Protecting your data with strong security
* Supporting your right to access, correct, delete, or transfer your data

We operate with **privacy by design** and **privacy by default** principles.

---

# **2. Legal Basis for Processing**

Under GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

### **2.1 Contractual Necessity**

To provide Palactix services (e.g., scheduling posts, managing accounts).

### **2.2 Consent**

For connecting social media accounts, enabling analytics, or using AI-powered features.

### **2.3 Legitimate Interests**

To improve user experience, monitor usage, and enhance platform performance.

### **2.4 Legal Obligation**

For complying with financial, tax, or regulatory requirements.

---

# **3. Your GDPR Rights**

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

### **3.1 Right to Access**

You can request a copy of the personal data we store about you.

### **3.2 Right to Rectification**

You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

### **3.3 Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”)**

You can request deletion of your personal data, unless we are legally required to keep it.

### **3.4 Right to Restrict Processing**

You can request limited use of your data.

### **3.5 Right to Data Portability**

You may request to download your data in a machine-readable format.

### **3.6 Right to Object**

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### **3.7 Right to Withdraw Consent**

You may withdraw consent for optional features or integrations at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **[support@palactix.com](mailto:support@palactix.com)**

# **4. Data Transfers Outside the EU/EEA**

Some of our service providers (e.g., hosting, email, analytics) may be located outside the EU/EEA.

When we transfer data to these providers, we ensure they have:

* **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)**
* **Adequate safeguards**
* **Strong security protections**

We do not transfer personal data to countries without proper data protection.

---

# **5. Data Retention**

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to:

* Provide our services
* Meet legal obligations
* Resolve disputes
* Enforce agreements

When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

---

# **6. Data Security Measures**

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data:

* Encrypted passwords
* Token-based authentication
* HTTPS security
* Restricted employee access
* Monitoring & auditing
* Secure cloud infrastructure
* Regular security assessments

No system is 100% secure, but we continuously work to strengthen our protections.

When you connect external platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, X, Slack, Jira, Monday, etc.):

* You choose the permissions
* We only access what you authorize

* Offer guidance on what users should do
